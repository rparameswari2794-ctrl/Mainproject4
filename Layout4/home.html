Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Do I need to be fit before joining?

›

Not at all! Our programs are designed for all levels — from beginners to advanced athletes. Trainers adjust workouts based on your ability.

2. What types of classes do you offer?

›

We offer a wide range: HIIT, Strength Training, Yoga, Pilates, CrossFit, Spin, Zumba, Boxing, and more.

3. Can I try a class before committing?

›

Yes! We offer a free trial class so you can experience our workouts before signing up.

4. What equipment do I need for online classes?

›

Most classes require just a mat and comfortable workout gear. Some strength classes may recommend dumbbells or resistance bands.
